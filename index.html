What Is CarboPrint?

Carbo Print is a website used for calculating our carbon usage. Carbon Print is nothing but the amount of carbon emitted by activities done by a person. This process requires a set of questions. It takes more than 40 factors, analyses all of them and tells the individual how much carbon they emit. It also takes the serial number of a devise and calculates the greenhouse gasses emitted by it on a daily basis.
