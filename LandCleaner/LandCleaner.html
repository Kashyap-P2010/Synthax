TERRA BOT

The terra bot is a land cleaning robot which uses Gps and sensors to locate its location and picks up trash accordingly.

• Terra Bot is an autonomous robot equipped with obstacle avoidance capabilities.

• It features an automated sweeper that collects trash into its onboard bin.

• Ultrasonic sensors help the robot detect and navigate towards trash.

• Upon reaching bin capacity, Terra Bot returns to its home base.

• At the home base, the robot automatically empties its bin and resumes its trash collection route.
