Aqua cleaner

Aqua cleaner and more about

• A lake bin with a spinning object creates a vortex that sucks in impurities (dust particles) from the lake.

• Once full, the bin is pulled out and emptied into a trash bin.

• Waste in the trash bin is segregated (plastic, paper, etc.).

• Segregated waste is then transported to a recycling plant for recycling.

• The emptied bin is returned to the lake for continued cleaning.
